MobSpoke Listen is an audio book store app that features full cast audio books

Communication between product visionary and product design was key for this project.

Significant attention was given to the cast selection feature. This key feature needed to be at the foreground of the product experience. The vision for the product is to engage audio book listeners in a new way.
